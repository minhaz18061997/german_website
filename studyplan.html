📅 Study Plan

80/20 weekly roadmap. 45–60 min/day consistently beats 5-hour weekend sessions.

A1  ·  0–3 months  ·  ~100 hrs total input

Learning Goals

GOAL: Survive basic conversations. • Core 300 vocabulary words • Verb conjugation (present tense) • Basic sentence structure (SVO) • Numbers, days, months, colours • Introduce yourself, order food, ask for help

Daily Resources

📺 Nicos Weg A1 (DW) – daily 🎧 Slow German Podcast – 3×/week 📖 DW A1 texts – daily 📱 Duolingo – 10 min/day 🗣️ italki tutor – 1×/week (30 min)

A2  ·  3–6 months  ·  ~200 hrs total input

Learning Goals

GOAL: Handle everyday situations. • Vocabulary to ~800 words • Past tense (Perfekt) for speech • Cases: Nominative, Accusative, Dative • Modal verbs fluently • Travel, shopping, directions, work basics

Daily Resources

📺 Easy German YouTube – 3×/week 🎧 Coffee Break German – 3×/week 📰 Nachrichtenleicht.de – daily 📚 Deutsch Perfekt magazine 🗣️ iTalki/Tandem – 2×/week

B1  ·  6–12 months  ·  ~350 hrs total input

Learning Goals

GOAL: Communicate independently. • Vocabulary to ~2,000 words • Subordinate clauses (weil, dass, wenn...) • Konjunktiv II for politeness • Passive voice basics • Express opinions, argue a point, tell stories

Daily Resources

📺 Easy German (no EN subs) – daily 📺 German TV: Tatort (with DE subs) 🎧 Nachrichtenleicht podcast – daily 📰 Zeit Online / Spiegel – 3×/week 🗣️ Language exchange – 3×/week

B2  ·  12–18 months  ·  ~500 hrs total input

Learning Goals

GOAL: Fluency for work & academia. • Vocabulary to ~4,000+ words • Complex sentence structures • Formal & written German registers • Konjunktiv I (reported speech) • Participate fully in native conversations

Daily Resources

📺 Native TV/Netflix DE – daily 🎧 Fest & Flauschig / NDR podcasts 📰 FAZ / Süddeutsche Zeitung – daily 📚 German novels (Hesse, Kehlmann) 🗣️ German-only conversation partners
